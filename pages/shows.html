“Enderas Masugui”

A surrealistic performance, in between the black comedy and the theater of the absurd. In just two square scene, the lives of all of us are unfolded, the lives were never lived…With the use of top table puppets and cinematic narration, the puppeteers animate the puppets without being visible to the audience
At its premier in October 2019, the performance won the firstprize for “Expressing Life with a Puppet” for the best animation performance in “Puppet is A Human Too” Festival at Theater Institute of Warsaw, Poland.
Duration: 60 min
No Text
Audience age: 15+
